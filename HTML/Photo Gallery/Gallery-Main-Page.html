Explore the dazzling world of Piltover and the gritty streets of Zaun, where steampunk tech and magic collide in a visually stunning journey of love, betrayal, and war. Witness the battle for control of powerful hextech, the rise of powerful and conflicted characters, and the deep-rooted political struggles that shape the fate of two cities. A tale of ambition, revolution, and the cost of progress, where no one is truly innocent, and every action ripples through the fabric of this fractured world.

Jinx, the chaotic and unpredictable mastermind of destruction, brings mayhem to both Piltover and Zaun with her relentless antics.

Vi, the determined enforcer of Piltover's justice, uses her fists to fight for the city's future, while struggling with her past.

Caitlyn, the sharp-minded sheriff, strives to bring order to the chaotic streets of Piltover, uncovering dark secrets along the way.

Jayce, the brilliant inventor and protector of Piltover, pushes the limits of science to safeguard his city from the rising threat.

Viktor, a visionary scientist whose belief in technology’s potential pushes him toward controversial choices, shaping Zaun's future.

Ekko, a brilliant inventor with the power to manipulate time, fights for the future of Zaun, seeking to change the course of history.

Heimerdinger, the brilliant scientist, works to ensure that Piltover’s future remains secure, even as dark forces threaten his city.

Silco, a ruthless figure in Zaun, believes in the power of rebellion and will stop at nothing to change the city's future.

Vander, a former enforcer turned leader, holds the fate of Zaun in his hands as he fights for survival and the future of his people.
