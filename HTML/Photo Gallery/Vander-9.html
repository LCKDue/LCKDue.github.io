VANDER



First Appearance: Episode 1 - *Welcome to the Playground* Vander, once a revered figure in Zaun, is introduced as a fatherly protector of the undercity. He is a former enforcer of the underworld, who has since retired to care for the orphaned children in Zaun, including Vi and Powder (Jinx). Despite his violent past, Vander becomes a symbol of hope and stability in a chaotic world, striving to protect his adopted family from the escalating tensions between Piltover and Zaun. His tragic fate in Episode 3 serves as a catalyst for the events that follow.
